# Tacharchy Brand Guide

## Colors

- **Primary:** Dark Orange `#FF6B00`
- **Background:** Dark `#0d1117`
- **Text:** White `#FFFFFF`
- **Muted:** Gray `#8b949e`
- **Accent:** Orange Glow `#FF8C33`

## Logo Concept

Greek letter **τ** (tau, first letter of ταχύς) as the main symbol:

- Bold, sharp geometric style — angular, fast-feeling
- The crossbar of the τ extends into a **lightning bolt** shape (representing speed)
- The vertical stroke could subtly incorporate the legs of the **∀** (anarchy A) at the base
- Single color: dark orange `#FF6B00` on dark background
- Minimal SVG, works at 16px favicon up to billboard size
- Clean sans-serif, no serifs

## Typography

- **Headers:** Bold, geometric sans-serif (Inter, Space Grotesk, or similar)
- **Body:** Regular weight, same family
- **Greek text:** ταχύς + ἀναρχία in the logo/brand, English everywhere else

## Style

- Minimal, sharp, no unnecessary decoration
- Feels fast, electric, rebellious but not chaotic
- More "performance engineering" than "punk rock"
- Think: F1 car livery, not graffiti

## Tagline

> **Fast Freedom.** Tacharchy gives back your freedom.
